THE ARTIST

Joshanne Daar has been recognized for her contribution to the art world.

Exhibitions

Current work is featured in Solo Exhibitions across the UK and wider Europe. This includes galleries such as Dublin Castle, Dublin, Galleria Nazionale d’Arte Moderna e Contemporanea, Roma and WIELS, Brussels.

Featured works in Group Exhibitions are on display across America, including Tilton Gallery, New York and Huntsville Museum of Art, Huntsville with other world leading artists.
